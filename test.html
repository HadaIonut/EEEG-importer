Gayel Wells is a youthful adult male human. He is relatively short and built like a wrestler, and has pale brown eyes and a dreadful beard, with translucent skin. The most notable physical trait of Gayel is that he has a sagging lower lip.

Gayel gossips about the most mundane things. When he is relaxed, he is driven. In moments of stress, he becomes compulsive. Religion-wise, Gayel is a casual observer.

Gayel is currently reasonably successful as a seamstress, with a background of being an urchin. He belongs to the peasantry social class.

Gayel currently has a black pearl worth 50gp in his pockets, and 40 Gold 7 Silver 7 Copper to his name.
